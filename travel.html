How to Visit Epicodus Island Resort

Getting to Epicodus Island Resort is easy. Here are some ways to reach us:

  • Fly: We are located near Epicodus Regional Airport. Catch a taxi from the airport directly to us.
  • Ferry: Catch the ferry from downtown Seattle, leaving every 5 minutes, 24 hours a day.
  • Kayak: We have a dedicated dock for personal kayaks and other small boats.
  • Swim: Self-explanatory.
